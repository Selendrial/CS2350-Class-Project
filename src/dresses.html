Dresses

I have several day dress patterns to choose from and I would be happy to make a dress or gown customized just for you!

Here is how it works:

  1. Review the sample dresses shown below. Decide what style you would like.
  2. Determine what color you are looking for.
  3. Contact me via the contact page to place your order. You will need to have the following meaurements ready: Your normal clothing size; your bust, waist & hip measurements; your arm length from the shoulder to the base of the thumb- this will determine sleeve length; measure from the base of your neck to your natural waist-this will determine how long the bodice will need to be. How long do you want the skirt to be? If you will be wearing the dress over a hoop it will have to be longer than if you were just wearing a petty coat. I can wither send the gown off hemmed to your specifications or leavit as is for you to hem after you have tried it on. I cannot stress the importance of accurate measurements. If you are not comfortable getting these measurements yourself, find someone who can help you. I am sure your local fabric/craft store would be willing to lend you a hand.
  4. Once the dress is completed (please allow 2-3 weeks) I will send you a photo for your approval. If you like what you see then payment arrangements will be made as outlined below.
  5. Pricing, Payment & Shipping: The cost for a dress is as follows: PRICING: Standard day dress - $50.00, Zuave Jacket and Skirt - $50.00, Fancier dresses $75.00 and up, Ridding Habits - $95 and up, Ball Gown $300.00 and up depending on the materials used. SHIPPING: Shipping will be based on the weight of the gown, insurance and your location. I will ship the item as soon as payment is received.
