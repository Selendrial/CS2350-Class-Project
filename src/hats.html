Hats

Each hat is hand made and may be either decorated or sent off to you as is for you to decorate. During the assembly process the hat begins to take on a personality of it's own and that is what guides me on how I decorate each one. I would love to make a custom hat for you.

Here is how it works:

  1. First, decide if you would like a completely finished hat or do you want just the base hat for you to decorate as you would like.
  2. Review the sample hats and dresses shown. This will help you to get an idea of what your perfect hat should look like. Decide what you like and don't like.
  3. Next choose your color or colors.
  4. Do you want a privacy veil, a long veil or perhaps no veil at all?
  5. Contact me via the contact page to place your order.
  6. Once the hat is completed (please allow 2-3 weeks) I will send you a photo for your approval.
  7. Pricing, Payment & Shipping: A base hat (undecorated) $30.00 plus shipping. A completed hat $40.00 plus shipping. Shipping will be based on the weight of the hat and your location. Payment can be made through PayPal, a cashier's check or money order. If you are outside the United States payment must be made through PayPal. I will ship the hat as soon as payment is received.
